# PureStream Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

## Introduction

PureStream ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Android TV application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Profile Information**: Names and profile settings you create within the app
- **Filter Preferences**: Profanity filter settings and custom word lists
- **Server Connection Data**: Plex server addresses and authentication tokens (stored locally)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: App performance data, crash reports, and feature usage (anonymized)
- **Device Information**: Android TV model, OS version, and app version for compatibility
- **Media Metadata**: Subtitle content for filtering purposes (processed locally, not transmitted)

## How We Use Information

- **App Functionality**: To provide core streaming and filtering features
- **Personalization**: To maintain your profile settings and preferences
- **Improvement**: To enhance app performance and fix bugs
- **Security**: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Profile data and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your personal data on external servers
- **Plex Integration**: Authentication tokens are transmitted securely to your Plex servers only
- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods

## Information Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your viewing habits or media library contents
- Transmit your custom filter words or profiles outside your device
- Access your Plex server data except for necessary app functionality

We MAY share anonymized usage data with:
- App analytics services (Google Analytics) for performance improvement
- Crash reporting services (Firebase Crashlytics) for bug fixes

## Third-Party Services

PureStream integrates with:
- **Plex Media Server**: For streaming content (governed by Plex Privacy Policy)
- **OpenSubtitles**: For subtitle downloading (governed by OpenSubtitles Privacy Policy)
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and updates

## Children's Privacy

PureStream is designed as a family-friendly application. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Profile information is stored locally and not transmitted to external services.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your profiles and data within the app settings
- Modify your filter preferences at any time
- Disconnect from Plex servers to remove authentication data
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

## Data Retention

- Profile data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- Crash reports and analytics are retained for 90 days
- Server authentication tokens are stored until you disconnect or change them

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates or notifications.
